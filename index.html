Everything you need to run your business

Invoicing & Quotations

Create professional invoices and quotations. Convert quotes to invoices or work orders in one click. Download PDFs and track statuses.

Work Orders & Tasks

Create job cards, assign tasks to workers, track completion with progress bars. Partial invoicing from completed tasks.

Inventory Management

Track stock levels per branch, adjust inventory, and view full movement history. Low‑stock alerts keep you restocked.

Customer & Price Lists

Manage customers with custom price lists. OTP‑protected price overrides keep sensitive pricing secure.

Multi‑Branch & Workspaces

Manage multiple branches under one organization. Switch between workspaces for separate businesses.

Team & Permissions

Invite team members with role‑based access. Owners and admins can assign workers, while viewers have read‑only access.

Chart of Accounts & VAT

Pre‑loaded Kenyan SME chart of accounts. Auto‑calculate VAT on invoices and purchases. KRA PIN and tax registration management.

Audit Logs & Security

Every change is tracked with full audit history. Soft‑deletes protect against accidental data loss. OTP verification for sensitive actions.
